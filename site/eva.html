Frequently Asked

What Is Eva-01? ▼
Eva-01 is your AI Chief of Staff. She's a system of Markdown templates, prompt libraries, and workflows configured entirely to you. She learns your personality, preferences, and priorities — then handles your calendar, inbox, operations, and follow-ups. She runs locally on your machine and integrates with your existing tools.
What AI Tools Does Eva-01 Work With? ▼
Any AI that accepts system prompts or file uploads. We recommend Claude Desktop (Projects) or ChatGPT (Custom GPTs). Eva-01 also works with local LLMs like Ollama, LM Studio, or anything that reads text prompts. The files are platform-agnostic by design.
Can I Customize Eva-01 After Download? ▼
Absolutely — that's the whole point. Every file is editable Markdown. Add new prompt templates, modify workflows, change your profile. You own the code. Fork it, extend it, make it yours. The structure is designed to be self-explanatory.
What Does Eva-01 NOT Do? ▼
Eva-01 does not write code, make marketing decisions, or make strategic business decisions for you. She's an operational assistant — she handles the admin, scheduling, communications, and follow-ups so you can focus on building. She surfaces information; you make the calls.
What If I Need Help After Installing? ▼
You get free access to our support chat agent, which can answer questions from our knowledge base instantly. If you need deeper troubleshooting or custom help, our support agent will route you to a human or use reasoning capabilities (paid per use, billed via Stripe).

Your Files, Your Rules

You Own Eva-01. Forever.

No accounts. No cloud lock-in. Just files on your machine that you control completely.

💾

Save It Anywhere

Eva-01 is a folder of Markdown text files. Save it on a USB drive, back it up to Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud — wherever you keep important things. It's yours like a document is yours.

🔐

Your Profile Is Inside the Files

Your name, personality, communication style, key relationships — it's all stored in ceo-profile.md. That's why you might want a secure USB or encrypted drive. Treat it like you'd treat a personal journal.

🔄

Reinstall Anytime

Lost your laptop? Got a new machine? Just load the files into your AI tool again. Eva-01's identity stays consistent because it's based on your profile template. She'll know you the same way.

📋

Active Work Resets — Identity Doesn't

If you do a fresh install, you'll need to re-enter current items: active projects, this week's priorities, open follow-ups. But Eva-01's personality — your communication style, schedule rules, key people — all carry over.

🔧

Use the Template Over and Over

Think of Eva-01 as a template, not an app. You can spin up fresh instances for different contexts — one for your startup, one for a side project, one for board prep. Same structure, different priorities.

👁️

Inspect Every Line

There's no compiled code, no binaries, no hidden logic. Every file is plain-text Markdown you can read in Notepad. If you want to know exactly what Eva-01 knows about you, open config/ceo-profile.md.

🔒

A Note on Security

Your Eva-01 files contain personal information — your name, your schedule, your key relationships. We recommend storing your download on an encrypted drive or a secure cloud folder with two-factor authentication enabled. We never see this data after you generate your files. Once it's on your machine, it's between you and your USB stick.
