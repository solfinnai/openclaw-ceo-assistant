# Investor Update Email

> Use monthly or as needed. Keeps investors in the loop without taking hours to write.

## Prompt

```
Draft my investor update for [Month/Period]:

Use this structure:
1. **TL;DR** — 2-3 sentences on where we are
2. **Wins** — What went well (be specific with numbers if possible)
3. **Challenges** — What's hard right now (be honest, investors respect transparency)
4. **Key metrics** — [Fill in your metrics: MRR, users, burn rate, runway, etc.]
5. **Asks** — What I need from investors (intros, advice, hiring help)
6. **What's next** — Focus for the coming month

Here's the raw info:
[Paste bullet points, notes, or just talk through it]

Write it in my voice. Keep it under 500 words. Investors are busy — respect that.
```

## When to Use

Monthly investor updates, or whenever the CEO needs to send a stakeholder communication.
