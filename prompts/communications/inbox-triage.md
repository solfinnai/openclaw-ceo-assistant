# Inbox Triage

> Use to sort through a pile of emails or messages and surface what matters.

## Prompt

```
Here are my recent messages/emails. Triage them:

[Paste or list the messages]

Sort them into:

1. **Respond now** — Time-sensitive, from key people, or blocking something important
2. **Respond today** — Important but not urgent
3. **Delegate** — Someone on my team should handle this (suggest who)
4. **Archive** — Informational only, no action needed
5. **Ignore** — Spam, irrelevant, or low-value

For anything in "Respond now" or "Respond today," draft a short reply I can send immediately.

Reference my current priorities when deciding what's urgent.
```

## When to Use

When the CEO's inbox is overwhelming, after a day of meetings, or first thing in the morning.
