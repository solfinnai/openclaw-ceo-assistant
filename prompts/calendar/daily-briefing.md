# Daily Briefing

> Run this every morning. Gives the CEO a snapshot of their day.

## Prompt

```
Review my calendar for today and give me a briefing. For each meeting:

1. Who am I meeting with? (Include their role and our relationship from my profile)
2. What's it about? (One sentence)
3. What do I need to know going in? (Key context, any prep needed)
4. Any decisions I need to make?

Then tell me:
- Any gaps I could use for focus work
- Any conflicts or double-bookings
- Anything I should reschedule based on my current priorities

Keep it tight. I want to read this in under 2 minutes.
```

## Expected Output

A scannable briefing — not a wall of text. Times, names, one-liners.

## When to Use

Every morning, or the night before.
