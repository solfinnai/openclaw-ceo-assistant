# Meeting Prep

> Use before any important meeting. Especially external ones (investors, partners, candidates).

## Prompt

```
I have a meeting coming up:

- **With:** [Name(s)]
- **Topic:** [What it's about]
- **Date/Time:** [When]
- **Context:** [Any background — e.g., "follow-up from last week's pitch," "first intro via [Referrer]"]

Prepare a one-page briefing:

1. **About them** — Who are they? What do they care about? Any recent news?
2. **Our history** — What's our relationship? Any past conversations or commitments?
3. **My goal for this meeting** — Based on my current priorities, what should I aim to get out of this?
4. **Suggested agenda** — 3-5 bullet points, with time estimates
5. **Watch-outs** — Anything sensitive, any landmines to avoid?
6. **Follow-up plan** — What should happen after this meeting?
```

## When to Use

Before investor meetings, board meetings, key hires, partner conversations, or any meeting where the CEO needs to show up sharp.
