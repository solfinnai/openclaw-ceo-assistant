# Weekly Review

> Run on Friday afternoon or Sunday evening. Closes out the week and sets up the next one.

## Prompt

```
Run my weekly review:

**This week:**
1. What meetings did I have? Summarize outcomes and any open follow-ups.
2. What commitments did I make? List them with deadlines.
3. What fell through the cracks? Anything I said I'd do but didn't?

**Next week:**
4. What's on my calendar? Flag anything that needs prep.
5. Based on my current priorities, is my time allocated well? Suggest changes.
6. What follow-ups should I send before Monday?

Give me a clean list I can act on in 15 minutes.
```

## When to Use

End of every week, or start of a new one.
